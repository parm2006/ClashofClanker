## Tutorial

1) Open up the file with AHK, this will display a gui
2) Check the farming and settings tab,(defualts are good for high townhalls, but might want to change for lower levels) ie(500k loot minimum for bases)
3) Start calibration the gui will maximuze the COC window and tell you what to click on
   - first hit the gold and elixir thresholds to trigger an upgrade or wall upgrade
   - then the builder icon at the top, and then then specific buttons for wall upgrade,
   - there is an issue with when you have very few walls left to upgrade since this removes the add 10 button,  instead of clicking on the center of the add and remove and gold and elixr buttongs, you should click where there is overlap between the buttons, Since i have finished my walls, i am not sure where exactly the buttons are. Also it might be different if you have wall rings(they add an extra button)
   - then click on war logo, attack, find a match, and attack, to find a base
   - then go over the gold and elixir loot numbers to the bot knows where to look, then hit the next and wait for the model to fix your screen, then select the start end end points for each of the 4 sides of the base,, then deploy just a single tropp so you can get the return home screen and select the center of it, once you press wait again for the model to fix your screen.
   - then you can select the collectors( it will collect about once an hour)
4) once main village calibrated do BB as well if you want, (it just spams attacks for first and second phase no upgrades)
5) bot should run now, make sure you toggle in farming upgrade walls on or off, and set your loot minimums in the gui settings.
